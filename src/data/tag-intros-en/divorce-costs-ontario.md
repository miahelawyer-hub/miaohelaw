**Money and process:** posts tagged **divorce costs Ontario** — how money is actually spent in family litigation (scope, disclosure, court steps, enforcement), not a rate card.

Talking about “the cost of divorce” without naming **what is in dispute** is almost meaningless. An uncontested desk file and a multi-issue trial can both be “a divorce,” but they do not share a budget. Ontario’s family system adds fixed costs — court fees, process servers, valuations — on top of lawyer time, and **disclosure fights** can outrun everything else.

Articles filed here tackle money questions head-on: how lawyers bill, why “simple” cases balloon, what FRO can and cannot do about arrears, and how to avoid paying twice because pleadings were filed in the wrong sequence.
