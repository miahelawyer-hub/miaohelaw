**Regional logistics:** posts tagged **GTA family lawyer** — regional sprawl and court logistics across the Greater Toronto Area; substance remains Ontario divorce and family statutes ([family law overview](/en/family-law/)).

Some clients live in three cities in one week: office downtown, kids’ activities in another municipality, parents in a third. Searching “GTA family lawyer” usually means you already know **mobility is part of the problem**, not a footnote. The Divorce Act and Ontario’s family statutes do not award points for traffic jams — but your schedule still affects parenting plans and urgency motions.

This tag collects writing that assumes **regional sprawl as normal**: how to think about court choice, why “where we filed last time” might not repeat, and how to keep support and parenting arguments from being collapsed into one improvised rule. It is broader than any single-neighbourhood intro and narrower than “all of Ontario.”
